Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about appointments, grooming, vaccination inquiries, location, and what to prepare before visiting.

Do I need an appointment?

Appointments are recommended for better scheduling, but you may message or call the clinic to ask about availability.

Do you offer pet grooming?

Yes. Please contact the clinic to confirm available grooming schedules.

Do you offer vaccination services?

Yes, vaccination services may be requested. Please message or call for schedule and availability.

Can I ask for service rates online?

Yes, message the clinic through Facebook or contact the listed numbers.

Where is the clinic located?

B1 L2 SEI Bldg. Sarmiento Townville, Poblacion 1, San Jose del Monte, Philippines, 3200.

What should I bring before visiting?

Bring your pet’s available records, vaccination card if available, and important details about your pet’s concern.

Do you accept emergency cases?

Please call or message the clinic directly to confirm current availability. The clinic does not claim 24/7 emergency service.
